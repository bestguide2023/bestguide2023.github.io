The next step is to enable AirPlay on your Android TV Box. This is done by going to the home screen of your Android TV and selecting “Settings”. Next, select “Network” and then “AirPlay”. Enable the AirPlay feature by sliding the switch to “on”.

The third step is to pair your iPhone and Android TV Box. On your iPhone, go to the AirPlay settings. Select your Android TV from the list of available devices. Enter the password that appears on the TV screen to confirm the pairing.

Finally, you can start sending files from your iPhone to your Android TV Box. In the AirPlay settings, select the “Send” option. Now select the file that you want to send and then tap “Send” to finish. Your file will now be sent to your Android TV Box and ready to view.
