Once your watch is connected to your phone, you are ready to go.

The third step is to add your custom ringtone to your watch. To do this, open the “Settings” and select “Ringtones”. Here you can select your custom ringtone and click “Apply”. Your new ringtone will now be set and you can test it out by pressing a button on the watch.

The last step is to make sure the new ringtone is saved. To do this, go back to “Settings” and select “Save Settings”. This will save all your settings including your new ringtone. Now your Samsung watch will play your chosen ringtone whenever you receive a call or notification. With this guide, you’ve now successfully set a unique ringtone on your Samsung watch.
