3. Troubleshooting Tips for Screen Sharing between Android and LG Smart TV#

Having trouble screen sharing between your Android phone and LG Smart TV? Here are some troubleshooting tips to help. First, make sure your devices are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Finally, double-check that your TV and phone are compatible with Screen Sharing. If not, make sure to download the specific Screen Sharing app for your LG Smart TV from the Play Store.
