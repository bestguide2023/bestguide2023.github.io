  3. Open the Files app and go to the “Downloads” folder.

  5. Once you have the screenshot, you can move it to a different folder or share it with others.

  6. To take a partial screenshot on your Chromebook tablet, press and hold “Ctrl-Shift-Show Windows Switcher” keys at the same time.

  7. The screen will dim again, and you can select the portion of the screen you want to capture and save.

3. Take a Partial Screenshot#

Taking partial screenshots can be very helpful if you are trying to annotate a large screenshot or you just want to focus on a particular area of the screenshot. To do this on your Chromebook Tablet, you can follow these steps:

  1. Press and hold the Ctrl, Shift, and the window switch key at the same time. This will capture the current window.

  2. Use the touch screen to draw a rectangle around the area you want to take a partial screenshot of.

  3. When the rectangle is drawn, a full screenshot will be taken and you will be able to crop it by dragging the edges of the rectangle.

  4. Once you are satisfied with the selection, press “Ctrl” and “Enter” at the same time to save the screenshot. You are now ready to share it with your friends or annotate it as you please.
