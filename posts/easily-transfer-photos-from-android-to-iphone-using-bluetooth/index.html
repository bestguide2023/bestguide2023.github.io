I. How to Transfer Photos from Android to iPhone via Bluetooth#

Transferring photos from your old Android phone to your new iPhone can be a hassle. Fortunately, you can use Bluetooth to make the process of transferring photos to your new device easier. Here is an easy guide on how to transfer photos from your old Android phone to your new iPhone using Bluetooth.

First, you’ll need to enable Bluetooth on both your Android and iPhone device. You can do this by navigating to the settings of the devices and then switching the Bluetooth to the ‘on’ position. After that, make sure that both devices are discoverable and that the devices are paired together.

Next, open the photo app on your Android device and select the photos you want to transfer. Then tap the share button and select the Bluetooth option. You will then be prompted to select the iPhone device to which the photos will be sent.

Once the transfer is completed, you’ll need to open the Photos app on the iPhone and tap the ‘Photo Library’ option to view the photos you have transferred. You can then choose to save them on the device or delete the photos if you don’t need them anymore.

That’s it! Now you can easily transfer photos from your old Android phone to your new iPhone device using Bluetooth. With a few easy steps, the process is over in no time.
