Second, you can also use the key combination “Alt” + “Print Screen” to take a screenshot of the active window only. This will also save the image file in the Pictures folder.

Third, if you only want to clip a part of your screen, press the “Windows Logo Key” + “Shift” + “S”. Fourth, you can paste the screenshot in the editable document of your choice. Just press “Ctrl” + “V” at the same time. This will paste the screenshot.
