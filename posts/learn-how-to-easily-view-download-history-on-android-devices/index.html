1. Steps to view download history on Android#

Do you need to know the history of all the downloads you’ve made on your Android device? This simple guide will show you how easy it is to view download history on Android devices.

First, open the Google Play Store app. You’ll find this app on the home screen or app drawer of your Android device. Once the Google Play Store app is open, tap on the Menu icon (three horizontal lines) located in the upper-left corner of the screen.

Next, select My Apps & Games. This will provide the details of all the apps you’ve downloaded from Google Play Store so far. From the list of apps, tap on the three-dot icon for the app for which you want to view the download history.

Now, select ‘View Download History’. This will show you when the app was downloaded and thus you can easily view the download history on Android devices.

Finally, you can uninstall the app if you want. To do so, simply go back to the list of apps and select the Uninstall button. That’s it! Now you know how simple it is to view download history on your Android device.
