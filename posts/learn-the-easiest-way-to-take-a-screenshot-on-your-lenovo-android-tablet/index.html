Finally, users with Lenovo tablets running Android 5.0 and above can use the Screenshot assistant tool. This tool is built into the device and makes taking screenshots easy. The tool is accessed by going to the Quick Settings menu and tapping on the Screenshot assistant option. Once the tool is activated, users can take screenshots by tapping on the Capture button.
