Q2: Are QR codes safe to use? A2: Yes, QR codes are generally safe to use since they are used to transfer small bits of data from a website or app. However, it is important to ensure you are scanning QR codes from secure sources, otherwise the data can be compromised by malicious actors.

Q3: Does my phone need a specific app to scan QR codes? A3: No, most modern Android phones come with a built-in camera app that also has a QR code scanner. If your camera app doesn’t have this feature, then you can download a QR code scanner from the Google Play Store.

Q4: What kind of data can QR codes contain? A4: QR codes can contain a variety of data such as URLs, contact information, appointment reminders, and more depending on the source. You can also create your own QR codes with custom content.

Q5: Can I scan QR codes from a distance? A5: Yes, some QR code scanners can scan QR codes from a distance, but it is usually best to scan the codes from up close in order to get the most accurate results.
