Taking screenshots of one-time photos sent or received via WhatsApp is simpler than you think. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

First, open the WhatsApp chat containing the one-time photo. Tap and hold the photo until a pop-up menu appears. From the options, select ‘Save Photo’. After selecting that, the photo will be automatically saved to your device’s gallery.

To take a screenshot, simply press the ‘Home’ and ‘Power’ buttons at the same time. When the screenshot appears in the lower left corner of your screen, it indicates that the screenshot has been successfully taken.

Once the screenshot has been taken, it can be found in the gallery of your device. To view the screenshot, open the gallery, where it will be saved as a .jpeg image. You can then open the screenshot to view it.

At this point, you have successfully taken a screenshot of a one-time WhatsApp photo. Enjoy!
