Q1. How can I connect my AirPods Pro to my Android device?

Q2. What should I do if my AirPods Pro won’t connect to my Android device?

A2. First, make sure that your Android device is compatible with Bluetooth 5.0. If yes, check if Bluetooth is enabled on your device. If it is, try resetting your AirPods Pro. To do this, press and hold the pairing button on the back of the case for at least 15 seconds. Then try connecting again. If this doesn’t work, try connecting your AirPods Pro to another device to check if it’s a device compatibility issue.
