Updating your Android TV version 4.4.4 is a straightforward process. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Next, click on the Install Update option and accept the Terms and Conditions that accompany it. The installation process will start and it may take some time to complete. It is advised that you keep your device connected to a power outlet during the process.

3. Troubleshooting Post-Update Problems#

Post-update problems can be a headache to deal with, especially if you’re not sure how to troubleshoot them. If you’ve just updated your Android TV 4.4.4 to the latest version, this step-by-step guide is here to help.

First, take stock of your installed apps and check if there are any that are no longer working. If so, then re-install them and see if that fixes the issue.

If the apps still don’t work, go to your TV’s Settings and look for Software Updates. Select it and see if there are any available updates available. If so, update them and see if that solves your problem.

If a software update doesn’t fix the issue, it’s time to restart your TV. To do so, press and hold the power button for a few seconds until it turns off. Then turn it back on and check if the problem is still there.

Lastly, check your internet connection. Slow internet or no connection at all can cause post-update problems. Check if your internet is working properly and try updating again if it isn’t.
