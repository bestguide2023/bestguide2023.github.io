1. Understand current Android version#

Android smartphones are constantly updated with the latest technology. To make sure you’re always enjoying the best experience with your Samsung M30s, you need to make sure you’re on the latest version. Upgrading your Android version is easy and this article will teach you how to do it.

First, make sure your device is connected to a stable Wi-Fi network. Then open your device’s Settings and navigate to About Phone. Next, tap Software Update and then Check for Updates. Your device will automatically begin searching for the latest Android version available. Once it finds the update, it will give you the option to download it.

Finally, tap Download Now. The download process may take some time depending on your connection. Once the download is complete, the installation process will start. Make sure to keep device connected to the internet until the process is complete. After the installation finishes, your device will restart into its new version.

Now that you know how to upgrade your Android version, you can make sure you’re always enjoying the best experience with your Samsung M30s. It’s a simple process that can keep your device running smoothly and give you access to the latest features.

3. Execute the steps to upgrade M30s#

Upgrading the Android version on any phone can be a tedious task. To make your life easier, following these step-by-step guide to upgrade your Samsung M30s from an earlier version of Android to the latest version.

First, make sure your device is charged up to at least 50 percent. After that, open the Settings app and click on the Software update tab. Check if an update is available for your device, if yes, then proceed to download and install it. The download might take a few minutes depending on your internet connection.

Once the download is complete, restart your device into recovery mode and select Apply update from the options provided. Your device will start to install the update and once done, it will restart. After that, you will have the latest version of Android installed on your device.

In case you don’t see any update in the Software update tab, don’t worry, you can still upgrade to the latest version of Android using a computer. Download the latest Android firmware for M30s from the manufacturer’s website and transfer it to your device. Restart your device into recovery mode and select Install update from SD card. Your M30s will start installing the firmware and once done, you will have the latest version of Android installed.

By following these steps, you should now be able to upgrade easily and quickly from an earlier version of Android to the latest version of Android on your Samsung M30s.
