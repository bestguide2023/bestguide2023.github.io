3. Transferring Files via Cloud Storage#

Transferring files from an iPhone to an Android tablet may seem like a daunting task, but it is actually easy to do. With cloud storage services like Google Drive, transferring files is simpler than ever. Here is a three-step guide to help you get started.

First, open the Google Drive app on both the iPhone and the Android tablet. Login with your Google account and grant permissions when asked. When both devices are connected to the same account, you can start transferring your files.

Next, select the files you wish to transfer and choose the ‘Add to Drive’ option. This will store the selected files to the Google Drive cloud.

Finally, open the Google Drive app on the Android tablet and locate the transferred files. Click the ‘Download’ icon to download the files from the cloud to the device. Once the download is complete, you will have successfully transferred files from your iPhone to the Android tablet.
