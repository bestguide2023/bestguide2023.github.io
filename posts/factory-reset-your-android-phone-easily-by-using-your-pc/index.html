Install the drivers into your PC, and plug your Android phone in with a USB cable. Secondly, download Android SDK tools from the Android developer website. Once unzipped, open ‘SDK Manager’ and select ‘Tools’ and ‘Android SDK Platform-tools.’

Thirdly, open a command prompt in the ‘platform-tools’ folder and type ‘adb devices’. This command will list all the currently connected devices. Type ‘adb reboot recovery’ to reboot your phone into recovery mode. Then type ‘adb factory reset,’ and the phone will begin to reset.

Finally, once the factory reset process is completed, type ‘adb reboot’ to reboot your phone and restart it normally. Following these steps should successfully factory reset your Android phone from your PC.
