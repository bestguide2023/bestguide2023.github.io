1. Steps to take screenshot on Windows Surface Laptop Go#

Taking screenshots on a Windows Surface Laptop Go can be really useful for a variety of purposes. Here are some easy steps to capture and save a screenshot on your Windows Surface Laptop Go. First, press the ‘Windows’ logo key and the ‘Print Screen’ key simultaneously. This will take a fullscreen screenshot of your device and save it to your Pictures folder. Second, if you instead prefer to capture only a specific area of your screen, you can press the ‘Windows’ logo key and the ‘Shift’ key at the same time, and then press the ‘S’ key. This will enable you to draw a box on your screen, select what you want to capture, and save it to your clipboard. Third, you can use the built-in ‘Snipping Tool’ for more precise cropping and editing capabilities. The tool will let you capture screenshots in different shapes or area size. Last, you can access a more powerful tool called the Snip & Sketch tool, which provides more advanced screenshot features. There you can take a fullscreen screenshot, capture pieces of the screen and annotate your screenshots with different colors, texts, and shapes.

2. Taking Screenshots on Windows Surface Laptop Go#

Taking a screenshot on a Windows Surface Laptop Go is a helpful way to capture your favorite images, conversations and more. Here are the easy steps to follow to take a screenshot on your Windows Surface Laptop Go. First, press the top volume button and the power button at the same time. This will create a shutter sound and the screen should dim for a few seconds. Second, you will be able to find the screenshot just taken in the Pictures folder in File Explorer, under Screenshots. If you would like to change the storage location, you may have to go to Settings > Ease of Access and then look for the option to set the Screenshots folder. Third, to capture a specific area of the screen, use the Windows key + Shift + S to open the Snip & Sketch tool. You can then use your mouse to highlight and select the area that you would like to capture. Finally, to open the Snip & Sketch tool manually, search for Snip & Sketch in the Windows search bar. With these tips, capturing a screenshot on your Windows Surface Laptop Go will be a breeze.

Taking screenshots on Windows Surface Laptop Go couldn’t be easier. Follow these simple steps to capture the perfect screenshot of your screen:

The second step is to take a screenshot of just a single window. To do this, press Alt + PrtScn at the same time. This will take a screenshot of the window you are currently using and save it to the “Screenshots” folder on your device.

You can also take a screenshot of a section of your screen. To do this, press Windows logo key + Shift + S. This will open a snipping toolbar which you can use to select and capture a specific area of your screen.

Finally, you can also take a screenshot of a specific application. To do this, press Alt + PrtScn at the same time, then go to the application that you wish to take a screenshot of and press Alt + PrtScn again. Q1. What is a screenshot?

A1. A screenshot, also known as a screen capture, is an image taken of whatever is being displayed on the computer screen. It can be used to capture an image of an entire screen or a specific area of the screen.

Q2. How can I take a screenshot on my Windows Surface Laptop Go?

A2. Taking a screenshot on your Windows Surface Laptop Go is easy. First press the Windows key and the Print Screen key at the same time. This will create a screenshot of your entire display and save it in your Screenshots folder. Alternatively, you can press the Windows key + Volume down button to capture a screenshot.

Q3. What is the best way to capture an active window on my Windows Surface Laptop Go?

A3. The best way to capture an active window on your Windows Surface Laptop Go is to press Alt + Print Screen on your keyboard. This will take a picture of the active window and save it in your Screenshots folder.

Q4. Are there other methods I can use to take a screenshot on my Windows Surface Laptop Go?

A4. Yes, you can also take a screenshot using the Snipping Tool. This tool is a built-in feature that allows you to capture and annotate a portion of your screen. You can access this tool by searching for it in the Windows Search Bar.

Q5. How do I open the screenshots I have taken on my Windows Surface Laptop Go?

A5. All your screenshots will be stored in the Screenshots folder which you can access by opening the File Explorer. You can open the screenshots saved in this folder with the Photos app or any other photo-viewing application.
