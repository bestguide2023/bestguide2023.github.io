Transferring contacts between an Android and iPhone device is a very simple process. All you need is your Android device, your iPhone, and a USB cable. Here are the steps to follow in order to successfully transfer your contacts:

First, connect your Android device and your iPhone to your computer with the USB cable. Make sure that your Android and iPhone are both connected to the same computer in order to continue.

Second, open up “My Computer” and locate your device in the list of external drives. Find the contacts folder in your Android and copy the contact files to a folder on your computer.

Third, connect your iPhone to the same computer and open the contacts folder. Drag and drop the contacts from the computer folder into the iPhone contacts folder.
