1. Overview of Taking Screenshots with Asus Chromebook#

Taking screenshots on an Asus Chromebook may seem tricky, but it’s actually surprisingly easy. You can capture an image of your entire screen, or just a portion of it. To make things even easier, you can use a keyboard shortcut to quickly capture your screen. Here’s a step-by-step guide to taking screenshots on your Asus Chromebook.

First, you need to open the application or window that you want to capture. Press “Ctrl” + “Switch Window” to take a screenshot of your entire screen. Once you press the shortcut, the screenshot will be saved to your Downloads folder. If you want to capture just a portion of the screen, press “Ctrl” + “Shift” + “Switch Window”. Your cursor will turn into a crosshair. Click and drag the crosshair over the area that you want to capture. When you release the mouse, the screenshot will be saved to your Downloads folder.

If you want to copy the screenshot to the clipboard instead of saving it to your Downloads folder, press “Ctrl” + “Shift” + “Ctrl” + “Switch Window”. This will copy the screenshot to the clipboard, where you can paste it into another application. If you’re using a touchscreen Chromebook, you can press “Ctrl” + “Search” + “Switch Window” to capture your screen.

Finally, if you want to save your screenshot as a file, press “Ctrl” + “Shift” + “S”. This will open the “Save As” window, where you can choose the format and location of your screenshot. Then click “Save”, and your screenshot will be saved as a file.

Using these keyboard shortcuts, you can easily take screenshots on your Asus Chromebook. Whether you’re capturing your entire screen or just a portion of it, you can quickly and easily get the images you need.
